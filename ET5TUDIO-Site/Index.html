Welcome to ET5TUDIO

ET5TUDIO, a new home recording studio, announces its official launch, catering to the needs of Electronic Dance Music (EDM) and Pop music producers. The studio specializes in cutting-edge sound design, offering a curated selection of presets for the popular synthesizer Serum 2, alongside a portfolio of original beats available for purchase.

With an emphasis on providing high-quality, contemporary sounds, ET5TUDIO aims to be a go-to resource for artists and producers seeking to enhance their productions with professional-grade audio assets. The available Serum 2 presets are meticulously crafted to fit the current trends in EDM and Pop, while the original beats provide a foundational spark for new tracks.
